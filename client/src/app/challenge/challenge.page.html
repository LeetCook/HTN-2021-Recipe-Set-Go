Spinach Kulcha Challenge Spinach Kulcha

Ingredients:

 Palak paste 1 cup Maida 1 tsp Oil 1/4 cup Boiled and mashed mutter 1tsp Green chilli - chopped 1 tsp Coriander leaves

Steps:

 Mix all ingredients of dough. Knead to semi soft dough. Keep aside for 2-3 hrs. Now take dough. Knead again for 2-3 mins Take a portion of dough and roll it. Put little stuffing and bind it and roll again Cook on medium flame on both sides by applying ghee till crispy Serve and enjoy hot with green chutney/yogurt/pickle /papad Start
